# Recognition Card Type

## Purpose
This card type tests your ability to recognize and read a Japanese word in its dictionary form.

## Required Fields
- **Word**: The dictionary form of the word.
- **Reading**: The reading of the word in `Kanji[Reading]` format.
- **Sentence**: An example sentence containing the word.
- **Pitch**: Pitch accent information.

## How to use
When you see the **Word** on the front, try to recall its reading and meaning. The back will reveal the details and provide a context sentence.
